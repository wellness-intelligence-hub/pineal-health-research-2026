## What We Cover

### 1. Environmental Exposure Research
Analysis of publicly available data 
on how environmental factors including 
fluoridated water supply affect 
long-term biological wellness markers.

**Key Research Areas:**
- Municipal water fluoridation studies
- Heavy metal bioaccumulation research
- Environmental toxin exposure mapping

### 2. Nutritional Support Protocols
Documentation of traditionally used 
and research-supported nutritional 
compounds for biological optimization.

**Featured Compounds:**
- **Chlorella:** Documented support 
  for the body's natural detoxification 
  process via heavy metal binding.
- **Tamarind Extract:** Traditionally 
  used in Ayurvedic practice to support 
  healthy fluoride excretion pathways.
- **Chaga Mushroom:** Adaptogenic 
  compound with documented immune 
  support and anti-inflammatory 
  properties.
- **Burdock Root:** Traditional 
  antioxidant compound used for 
  centuries in blood purification 
  protocols.
- **Purified Iodine:** Essential 
  mineral supporting thyroid function 
  and metabolic health markers.

### 3. Mindfulness and Cognitive Research
Publicly available research on the 
relationship between biological 
wellness markers and cognitive 
clarity, intuition, and mindfulness 
practice outcomes.
